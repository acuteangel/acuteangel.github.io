About

Welcome! My name is Angel Aquas, I work as a game developer for GungHo Online Entertainment, America. I'm currently working as a tech designer, but have been a software engineer at GungHo for the past 3 years.

I am currently located in Los Angeles, but I'm originally from Sacramento, California.

I graduated from University of California, Los Angeles in 2015 with a BS in Mathematics. After graduating I took a 6 month Full-Stack Web Development course through UCLA Extension.
